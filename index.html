Who Am I?

Professional Fullstack Developer with 5+ Years Experience

I am Ibrahim Aminu, also known as Prince Ij - a passionate Fullstack Developer and Software Architect with expertise in modern web technologies. My journey into software development began during my teenage years with SoloLearn, but everything changed when I enrolled in the ALX Software Engineering Program - an intensive one-year course that opened my eyes to what real development truly is.

I learned that software engineers don't just write code; we build the systems that power the world. This realization drove me to continuously expand my knowledge and skillset, transforming from a curious beginner into a professional developer capable of creating scalable, robust solutions using technologies like React.js, Node.js, Python, MongoDB, and TypeScript.
